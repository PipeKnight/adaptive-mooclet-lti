Please go to this URL hyperlink: ___[Add URL to Question here]___
Once you have successfully logged into Canvas and are able to see the Student View of a Question, part A of your request will be automatically submitted. Then, go to {{ request.get_host }}{% url 'engine:collaborator_request' %} This will provide you with information you can copy/paste and email to joseph_jay_williams@harvard.edu. Please also cc me.
Once part B of your request is matched against part A and approved, we will both receive confirmation by email.
You can then go back to the URL hyperlink, and you will see both the Student View and the Collaborator View of the Question, which will allow you to suggest and edit explanations.
